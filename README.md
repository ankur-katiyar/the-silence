# Silence

**Silence** is a short story written by **Ankur** on **May 26, 2003**.

The story explores how grief, loss, and unresolved trauma can slowly transform a person, and how a moment of humanity can restore what violence could never destroy.

## Description

A young man loses his mother in riots and is drawn into violence seeking revenge. Years later, during a mission, an encounter with innocent children and the death of his companion awaken his buried humanity, finally breaking the silence of hatred and bringing him peace.
